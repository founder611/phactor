About pHactor Premium Beverages

Kerala's premium packaged beverages, trusted internationally

pHactor is a premium bottled and packaged drinking water and beverage brand headquartered in Ernakulam, Kerala, India. Founded and led by CEO Sujith Renjith, the company was built on a simple idea: bottled beverages should be as reliable as the businesses that depend on them every day — hotels, hospitals, offices, and homes.

Searching for packaged drinking water, mineral water, fruit-flavoured soft drinks, or soda water in Ernakulam and across Kerala? pHactor offers a comprehensive range of beverages, including aerated and non-alcoholic options, with home delivery, hospitality supply, and export-ready packaging. Our facility follows a strict source-to-seal process, ensuring every bottle carries the same lab-verified purity and consistent quality.

As an internationally minded beverage brand, pHactor is positioning Kerala-made products for shelves and hospitality partners beyond India, combining local sourcing discipline with the packaging and documentation standards that export markets expect.

Our Process

Four steps from source to seal

The same discipline, batch after batch, so quality is never left to chance.

01 · Source

Protected natural intake

Water is drawn from monitored, protected sources selected for naturally low contaminants.

02 · Filter

Multi-stage filtration

Reverse osmosis and micron-level filtering remove impurities while preserving character.

03 · Balance

Mineral calibration

Calcium, magnesium and potassium are reintroduced for a clean, balanced taste.

04 · Certify

Batch lab verification

Independent testing confirms every batch before a single bottle leaves our Ernakulam facility.
